Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

Nivana (“we”, “us”, “the App”) stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information off-device.

1. Data Stored Locally

  • Meditation history (sessions, streaks, total minutes)
  • Program progress
  • Journal entries
  • User settings (theme, notifications, favorites, audio volumes)

2. No Third-Party Sharing

  • No analytics, advertisements, or crash-reporting SDKs are included by default.
  • No cloud backups or data syncing.

3. Your Control

  • Everything stays on your device; you can delete any item at any time.
  • Your data’s security depends on your device lock and encryption.

4. Policy Changes

If we ever add features that transmit or share data (e.g., cloud backup, analytics), we will update this policy, revise the “Last updated” date, and notify you within the app.
